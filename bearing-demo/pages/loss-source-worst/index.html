How a ransomware attack costs $1,049,200 in the worst case scenario

In the worst case scenario, the ransomware attack takes around a month to actually present itself after initial infection.

There are strains of ransomware that behave in this manner. In the worst case scenario, when Juice Shop attempts to recover data from the backups, they find they're also encrypted and are thus unable to recover data from the backup. This is a significantly catastrophic event to the organisation.

The assumptions to this scenario are:

  • All 250 desktop PCs are infected with ransomware; and
  • The 3 teams completely lose the ability to be productive whilst recovery is on-going.
